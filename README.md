# 🎬 Netflix Content Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Netflix Content Intelligence Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Netflix's content library and uncover valuable insights about movies and TV shows.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of Netflix content based on content type, ratings, genres, production countries, release years, and content duration trends.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze the distribution of Movies and TV Shows.
- Identify the most popular content ratings.
- Discover top content-producing countries.
- Explore trending genres on Netflix.
- Study content release trends over the years.
- Provide business insights through visual analytics.

## 🔍 Dashboard Features

### 1. Content Type Analysis
- Distribution of Movies vs TV Shows.
- Percentage contribution of each content type.

### 2. Rating Distribution
- Analysis of content ratings such as:
  - TV-G
  - TV-Y7
  - PG
  - R
  - TV-MA
  - Others

### 3. Genre Analysis
- Top genres available on Netflix.
- Identification of the most popular content categories.

### 4. Country-wise Analysis
- Countries contributing the highest amount of content.
- Comparison of content production across regions.

### 5. Release Year Trends
- Growth of Netflix content over time.
- Historical content release patterns.

### 6. Duration Distribution
- Comparison of movie durations and TV show seasons.
- Trend analysis across years.

## 📌 Business Insights

- Movies constitute approximately 70% of Netflix's content library.
- TV Shows account for around 30% of total content.
- Stand-Up Comedy and Romantic Movies are among the most popular genres.
- Content production has significantly increased after 2015.
- Multiple countries contribute heavily to Netflix's content ecosystem.
